Harry Potter

About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a wizard who lives in the magical world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is the "chosen one" and with the help of his friends takes down the dark wizard Voldemort.

The series

The Harry Potter series consists of seven fantasy novels written by J.K. Rowling.The books are called: philosopher's stone, chamber of secrets, prisoner of azkaban, goblet of fire, order of the phoenix, half-blood prince, and deathly hallows.
